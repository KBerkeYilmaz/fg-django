RED GROUP

The Red Group was built as a structure that examines the basic structures of countries by conducting research, prepares reports in line with the data it has obtained, and provides the necessary connections and equipment to get a share from the selected markets by taking these reports into consideration. The first step in research is the history of the countries. Entering a market that you do not know about its history, culture and ethnic structure is one of the biggest mistakes that can be made. Researches include projects planned or realized on a country basis, investments, import and export rates, signed agreements, relations with neighboring countries and many other structures. FIORES is not a political project, it does not and will not do politics.

BLUE GROUP

Working on health technologies, the Blue group works to develop different solutions for 3D modelling in the health sector.
